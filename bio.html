Hello! I am a 4th year undergraduate at the School of Arts and Sciences at the University of Pennsylvania. I am majoring in biochemistry and submatriculating in chemistry with a minor in sociology.

I was born in Incheon, South Korea but lived in Jeju Island until my family moved to Woodside, New York in August of 2001.

Since then, I have lived my entire pre-college life in New York City. I managed to gain entrance into the Bronx High School of Science where I discovered my passion for biology and chemistry.

I was accepted into the Macaulay Honors College at Hunter College- a fantastic program offering excellent financial aid and academic support.

It is there that I discovered my interest in structural biology.

Peep them white socks...

They're totally on purpose...

After my freshman year at Macaulay, I transferred to the College at the University of Pennsylvania because it offered more opportunities and resources for structural biology research.

I am current working in Dr. Ronen Marmorstein's lab on the MYST framily of histone acetyltransferases.

Check out some of what my mentor and I worked on, here!


Lab picnic photo! July 22, 2016

Apart from research, I am a strong believer in the benefits of community service.

I am the Deputy Director of Penn's branch of Innoworks, where we offer a free one week hands-on science camp that is targeted towards under-privileged middle-school-aged children.

I am also a section leader at the West Philadelphia Tutoring Project, which offers one-on-one weekly tutoring sessions and an opportunity to form a genuine relationship with a tutee.


My friends and I took our biology teacher out for dinner!

I am an avid traveler, especially to places with cultures that I might not be accustomed to. I love learning, hands-on, about the diversity of human culture and ingenuity. My travels to West and East Africa have instilled in me, a wider, worldly view.

Another hobby of mine is playing the piano and the clarinet. I've been playing the piano since I was 6, and the clarinet since I was in 6th grade. I am a member of the Penn Band, which everyone should definitely join!

I am also a beginning coder. I have taken an introductory computer science class at Penn as a sophomore in Java. During the summer of 2016, on top of my biochemistry research, I learned HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Git, and Bash shell programming to develop this website! Send me feed back here!
